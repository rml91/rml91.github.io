About

I am an undergraduate student in physics and mathematics at the University of St Andrews, with a strong interest in theoretical physics and its mathematical foundations. My studies focus on analysis, topology, and quantum mechanics.

I am particularly interested in gravitation and quantum field theory, with an emphasis on geometric approaches. My recent work includes topics such as conformal gravity and holography, as well as operator-theoretic aspects of quantum mechanics.

More broadly, I am interested in understanding how physical theories arise from underlying mathematical structures. I intend to pursue graduate study in theoretical physics.
